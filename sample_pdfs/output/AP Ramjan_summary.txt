This is a government order issued by the Ministry of Finance of Bangladesh, promoting Mr. Ramzan Ali, a Computer Operator in Grade 9, to the post of "Assistant Programmer" effective from the date of joining. The order outlines the terms of his promotion, including salary scale, benefits, and probationary period. 

Mr. Ramzan Ali's promotion is subject to a one-year probationary period as per the Government's Computer Personnel Recruitment Rules, 2019. His performance and conduct during this period will be evaluated, and the authorities reserve the right to terminate his service if found unsatisfactory. Upon successful completion of the probationary period, his appointment will be made permanent.

The order also mentions that any matters not explicitly covered in this order will be governed by prevailing government laws, orders, rules, and regulations. The order is effective immediately.
